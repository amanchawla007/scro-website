DESIGN AND FABRICATION

The Design and Fabrication team deals with the Design, Structural, and Propulsion aspects of the UAV. Their objective is to design and fabricate a frame to fit all the required components and payload of the UAV within the given weight and cost constraint. Proper materials should be selected to ease out the fabrication process without compromising on strength to weight ratio. The team is also responsible for the motor and propeller combination required to lift the UAV from the ground. Hence, it works closely with the flight system domain for the appropriate battery size to get the most flight time possible

FLIGHT SYSTEM

UAVs and drones require hardware and software elements that allow the aircraft to be controlled remotely either directly by a pilot or autonomously by an onboard computer. The whole packaged system is known as Flight system which assists and guides the drone's flight path, with status monitoring achieved from a ground control station.

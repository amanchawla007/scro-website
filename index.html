WHAT IS SCRO?
STUDENT COPTER RESEARCH ORGANIZATION
SCRO, which stands for Student Copter Research Organization, is a dynamic student-based research team under the aegis of SRM Institute of Science and Technology that develops various aviation technologies. Students from various backgrounds, branches, skillsets and expertise levels come together to inspire innovation and tackle real-world problems scientifically under the domains of Corporate, Computer Vision, Flight Systems, Web Development & Design & Fabrication.
To evolve, expand and establish copter technologies that are state-of-the-art and a result of an honest and strong commitment to the principles of inventive and innovative scientific approaches. To vigorously establish a strong presence in the technical community and build a successful organization that transforms copter research with robust qualitative output and a rich community by building on a resources, hubs and networks for young entrepreneurs and enthusiasts in the tech community. To participate, engage, and succeed in all levels of competitions, dissertations, conferences and consortiums ranging from intra-college, inter-college, state, national, continental and international and strive to secure the topmost spot in each of them and transform SCRO into a widely respected authority in the field. To coactively incentivize and encourage individuals and organizations in the scientific copter and UAV community by building on resources, hubs and networks.
Our vision lies in the best possible use of sustainable and futuristic technology as we comprehensively aim to assimilate the best technical minds under one roof and consolidate a strong foundation of committed individuals. We believe that our technological footprint is the true testament of our commitment to research and betterment.
OUR WORK
● To design, brainstorm, innovate and develop copter technologies that are state-of-the-art and a result of an honest and strong commitment to the principles of inventive and innovative scientific approaches;

DOMIANS

COMPUTER VISION

The Computer Vision domain makes the two ends meet: the computer software, through the eyes of the drone. We extract, analyse and understand useful information provided by digital images and videos captured by various image acquisition devices.

DESIGN AND FABRICATION

Design and Fabrication is responsible for the Structural and propulsion aspect of the drone and also its Fabrication. Our job is to make the drone light and sturdy.

FLIGHT SYSTEM

The motors and propellers are the drone technology, which move the UAV into the air and to fly in any direction or hover. FLIGHT SYSTEM domain work to design and develop flight and electronic systems as per requirement.

CORPORATE

SCRO Corporate works on various business fields such as Sponsorship and Partnerships, Marketing, Event Management, Creatives, etc.. to enhance the operations of the Organization.

WEB DEVELOPMENT

Web development domain provides a major support in deploying, presenting and depicting the necessary conceptual details and displaying the statics or visuals on a web or a portal with special links to drones.

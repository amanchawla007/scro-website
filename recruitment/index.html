Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is SCRO? What is the organization about and what are its areas of activity?

SCRO stands for Student Copter Research Organization. SCRO is a student based team of techno-enthusiasts who work in a student club format in the area of aviation science and technology. Our domains span various disciplines of what goes into successfully making a multi-copter or aviation related prototypes and designs.

What are the different domains at SCRO?

Design and Fabrication, Flight Systems. Web Development, Computer Vision, and Corporate. The domain names are pretty self-explanatory about their tasks and duties.

What exactly is Corporate? What kind of competencies does it require?

Corporate is the management and organizational backbone of the whole team. In corporate, we attend to various requirements such as social media outreach, public relations, sponsorships, content generation, team profile, event management and a variety of other tasks. Unlike other domains, Corporate will demand your soft skills such as leadership, team management, practical and critical thinking, business strategy, content creation, and effective marketing.

I am not sure about my strengths and skills and the extent to which I am good at my interests. Should I apply?

You should always apply. At SCRO, everyone is together to learn, mutually develop, gain proficiency in existing skills and develop a strong background. We don’t need expertise: you can develop that here. To work in SCRO, all you need is an honest and sincere passion towards your interests and a commitment to work with diligence and dedication. The journey matters!

Am I doing an internship with SCRO?

Yes and no. SCRO is not some corporate-tier company that has employees on a payroll, it is a student-led and student-based research organization that follows the lines of a student club where dedicated and willing enthusiasts come together to make change and create things. If selected, you will be a trainee for an interim period of three months. After that, you’ll become a part of the core team based on your work ethic and output.

Where is the member base of SCRO from?

The eligibility criterion for being a member of the team is that you should be a student of SRM Institute of Science and Technology, and within that, only from Faculty of Engineering and Technology.

What is the work culture at SCRO like? Will I be allowed to explore other interests?

At SCRO, we care deeply about creating and curating a fulfilling experience for our team members. There is no strict pyramidal hierarchy at all: we believe in and maintain a free and fair flow of ideas and interactions in the team. Everyone is free to bring up whatever they feel is relevant. The work culture is flexible and you won’t feel constrained, as if you have to give board room presentations in a room full of executives. At SCRO, your comfort, freedom and compatibility is taken care of. You are also free to explore your interests within the ambit of the team and you don’t have to stick to what you signed up for. Volunteer, work, lead and grow.

What should I expect to receive from the team? Are there any perks and benefits associated?

Often, the first thing that gets floated around when people think of joining organizations is experience. But here at SCRO, a major hallmark is the standard of experience that you shall receive. You will learn, incorporate and employ various soft skills apart from finding a platform for your technical talents and SCRO will provide a stage on which you can furnish, develop and refine your strengths as you work in the team. You will have wholesome exposure to a wide avenue of different opportunities.

How should I prepare for the recruitment?

There is no strict or rigorous preparation expected from you. Apart from being active and responsive when needed when we reach out to you, be authentic and consolidate your strengths. You may be asked to showcase your work and have an interview with us. Apart from that, there is nothing additional that has to be done on your part.

SCRO is a dynamic student-based research team under the aegis of SRM Institute of Science and Technology that develops various aviation technologies.

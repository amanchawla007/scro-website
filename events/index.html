PAST WORKSHOPS

AI, ML & Topology Workshop

A workshop was organised by SCRO at national level tech management fest AARUUSH 2019. “Generative Drone Design and Drone Detection “ was the subject matter on which the workshop was conducted. It was a two-day workshop headed by the SCRO team members, Robin Singh Balyan and Srivatsan. Day 1 had the main emphasis on Object Detection, Drone Detection, Face Detection and face recognition using AI and ML. The day 2 revolved around topology optimization which was the genuine purpose of the workshop. The workshop ended on a high note and the attendees now had a clear idea about what topology optimization was and why it's a trend in general product designing.

Quadcopter Championship

SCRO conducted the competition, Quadcopter Championship during the National level tech management fest AARUUSH 2019. The objective of the championship was to design a wireless remote controlled Quadcopter to pass through the arena to the drop point. The criteria for winning the championship was based on 3 points, the time taken by the quadcopter to complete the arena, the number of checkpoints passed, and if the drone drops a kit while passing an obstacle, then only the points for crossing the obstacle would be given, not on handling the payload of the drone. It was a championship which tested how efficient the quadcopter was and how great the team members handled the working of the quadcopter avoiding the breakthroughs with both speed and balance!

Data Science & Machine Learning Workshop

Student Copter Research Organization successfully administered its "Data Science and Machine Learning Applications and Opportunities" episode with Mr. Vaibhav Verdhan, principal Data Scientist at Johnson & Johnson, as the Guest Speaker. The session resonated well with the audience with a brief introduction on Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, it's different use cases, algorithms like supervised, unsupervised and semi-supervised and starting a career in Data Science as the pivot of discussion. In this 'Data Science made easy' Workshop, Mr. Vaibhav Verdhan emphasized on how to launch oneself in modern day technology spectrum. With all that said and done, we don't plan to stop with this and commit to more such informative workshops.
